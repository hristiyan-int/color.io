## Description

**Short Description (170 characters for Google Play):**
Extract stunning color palettes from photos! Capture, create, and share beautiful color schemes with an intuitive camera-based palette extraction tool.

**Full Description:**

Color.io is the ultimate color palette extraction and creation tool for designers, artists, and color enthusiasts. Transform any photo into a beautiful, harmonious color palette with just a tap.

**KEY FEATURES:**

**Camera & Photo Extraction**
- Point your camera at anything and instantly extract dominant colors
- Import photos from your gallery for palette creation
- AI-powered color extraction using advanced Median Cut + K-Means algorithms

**Smart Color Tools**
- Get color names automatically identified
- View colors in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats
- Calculate color contrast ratios for accessibility
- Generate complementary, analogous, and triadic color harmonies

**Palette Management**
- Save unlimited palettes to your library
- Edit, rename, and organize your color collections
- Add or remove colors with intuitive gestures
- Search and filter your palette library

**Export & Share**
- Export as PNG, PDF, CSS, SCSS, Tailwind, ASE, or JSON
- Share palettes directly to social media
- Copy color codes to clipboard instantly

**Community Features**
- Browse trending palettes from the community
- Follow your favorite creators
- Like, comment, and save inspiring palettes
- Tag palettes for easy discovery

**Perfect For:**
- Graphic designers creating brand color schemes
- Interior designers matching room colors
- Artists finding color inspiration
- Web developers building design systems
- Fashion enthusiasts matching outfits
- Anyone who loves beautiful colors!

Download Color.io now and start creating stunning color palettes!
